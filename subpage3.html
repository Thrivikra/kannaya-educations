HUGE LABS OF BIO AND CHEMICAL COURSES

May 22, 2025 by kannaya institutes

Biochemical and Chemical Engineering laboratories are specialized facilities designed to support research and education in the fields of biotechnology, chemical processes, and materials science. These labs are equipped with advanced instruments and technologies to facilitate a wide range of experiments and studies.
Biochemical Engineering labs focus on the application of biological sciences to the development of processes and products. They are equipped with tools and instruments to study and manipulate biological systems.
